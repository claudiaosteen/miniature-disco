HOW MUCH CAN YOU GROW?

We've calculated that a 2x1m wall can fill a family of 2's vegetable requirements for a month, which roughly translates to 6-10kg of fast-growing leafy vegetables like spinach, kang kong, lettuce, bok choy etc.

The One Kind Block offers food independence at a very affordable price.

GROW FROM ANYWHERE

The OKB's unique back attachment allows you to hang it anywhere in any home. We've tested it on windows, grills, balconies, walls, and gates.

It doesn't take up any walkway space along HDB corridors or condominium hallways, so don't worry, neighbors won't complain.

For windows or grills, use the zipties supplied in your One Kind Block set. For walls, you can use adhesive wall hooks (that support 3-5kg), nails or just plain adhesive tape.

One Kind Block really is a plug & play urban farming system. That means the only limit to how many blocks you can have is your imagination. Then simply buy the seeds and hydroponic solution from nurseries or online and start growing.
